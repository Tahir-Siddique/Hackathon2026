Pipeline

Assets → Normalise → Match NVD → EPSS+KEV → Rank → CSV / UI

  • Normalise — fuzzy CPE mapping (hardest step)
  • Match — filter ~23k CVEs to your stack (~6 s)
  • Rank — KEV first, then urgency / EPSS / CVSS
  • Criticality — plain-English severity badge

Ranking & criticality

Sort order

  1. KEV (yes first)
  2. Urgency: 40% CVSS + 60% EPSS (+KEV boost)
  3. EPSS → CVSS

Criticality badge

Critical KEV + high EPSS or CVSS ≥ 9

High KEV or CVSS ≥ 7 or EPSS ≥ 0.5

Medium / Low — lower scores
